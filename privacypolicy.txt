## StarWatch: Privacy policy

Welcome to the ScienceExplorer app for Android!

I have written no code to collect any personally identifiable information. The app aggregates Astronomy, Physics and other Youtube science channels and presents them to the user.

### Explanation of permissions requested in the app

The list of permissions required by the app 
    android.permission.INTERNET
    android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE
    android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
    android.permission.CHANGE_NETWORK_STATE

android.permission.INTERNET -- The app needs to connect to Youtube servers to fetch images. 
android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, CHANGE_NETWORK_STATE -- The app tracks network availability. If the network is unavailable some functionality must be turned off.
